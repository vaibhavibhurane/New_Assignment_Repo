About Us

Welcome to our world of unforgettable experiences! BharatYatra Holidays & Resorts India, a part of Leisure and Hospitality sector of the BharatYatra Group, offers quality family holidays primarily through vacation ownership memberships and brings to the industry values such as reliability, trust and customer satisfaction. The company's flagship brand ‘BharatYatra’, today has over 80,000 members , who can holiday at 100+ resorts in India .

We are passionate about creating memories that last a lifetime. With access to 100+ resorts in India, we offer a diverse range of destinations for your perfect getaway. No two holidays will ever be the same with us. Whether you prefer to relax at your favorite destinations or explore new ones, we have something for everyone. Our goal is to make every vacation magical and unique.

Our commitment to providing exceptional hospitality is reflected in our handpicked resorts, each offering a blend of luxury, comfort, and adventure. From stunning accommodations to world-class spa facilities, we ensure that every aspect of your stay exceeds your expectations. As you plan your magical holiday, rest assured that we are continually adding more exciting destinations to our portfolio. Your dream vacation is just a click away. Join us on a journey where every destination becomes a cherished memory. Discover the joy of travel with BharatYatra.
